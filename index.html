QRCrypt

  1. Cipher:
    • Type your message above.
    • Click Go to generate the ciphered message and key.
    • Copy and save the ciphered message.
    • Download QR or copy the key.
  2. Decipher:
    • Paste the ciphered message into the message box.
    • Scan, upload, or paste the key from the QR code.
    • Click Go to recover your original message.
